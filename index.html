Who am I?

My name is Damilola Aleje. I’m a Media personality and Creative Producer based in Lagos.

I am passionate about sharing unique stories about Africa. I am a qualified Media Proffessional who has been working actively in the media and creative space for over four years. I have worked with several broadcast outfits including NTA 10 Lagos, Nigeria info 99.3, online TV platform Happenings Tv as a Tv Presenter and Producer. I have worked with Film and Documentary production companies as a production manager and producer. I have contributed in creating contents for short films, documentaries and feature length and most of my projects are available on various Streaming Platforms and festivals across the continent.

As a media professional who is passionate about production, I am dedicated as a Media personality, Production manager and Creative producer to continue telling African stories across the African continent.
