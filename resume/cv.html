Projects

Team Lead/Producer; life at Takwabay Community Journalism project

I led a team to film a documentary on the living conditions of people living in Takwabay bay community in Lagos State. The people in the community have never experienced electricity since inception, they do not have good schools and lack teachers because of how far it is from the main city. Lack of transportation and proper waste management system. The project was done to create awareness about the community through our Community Journalism course in school during my final year.

Project Lead: Creative Mornings

Creative Mornings is an international free morning monthly talk series for creatives with presence in over 206 cities in the world. Nigeria, got her chapter in January 2019, where we host creatives from different walks of life to a monthly talk series which is aimed at helping them find their inner creativity and becoming better versions of themselves. We bring different speakers monthly to head the talk, which I host on a monthly basis and also source for speakers for our audience to interact with.

Media lead: walk against Suicide Campaign

Walk against Suicide campaign is a charity Organization "which I volunteered to help walk against the high right of suicide in Nigeria," so as for those who are depressed to seek help and never keep silent. Suicide was a strange phenomenon in Nigeria until recently and to put and end to this I decided to contribute my quota by creating awareness through the media. As well as get people involved in the project.
